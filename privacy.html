Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Communik ("we", "us", or "our") operates a unified social inbox platform that connects Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram, and TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Social account data: Access tokens and page/account identifiers for connected social channels (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram, TikTok).
  • Message data: Messages received and sent through connected social channels, for the purpose of displaying them in your unified inbox.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps, and IP addresses for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Communik service.
  • To display messages from your connected social accounts in a unified inbox.
  • To send messages on your behalf through connected channels.
  • To generate analytics and statistics about your messaging activity.
  • To improve the platform and fix technical issues.
  • To communicate service updates and important notices.

3. TikTok Integration

When you connect your TikTok account, we request access to the following permissions:

  • user.info.basic: To display your TikTok username and profile picture.
  • video.list: To display your TikTok content within the platform.

We do not share your TikTok data with third parties. TikTok access tokens are stored securely and used only to perform actions you explicitly request. You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time from the settings page.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below. Message history from connected social channels may be subject to retention policies of those platforms.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords (bcrypt), and secure token storage. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Disconnect any social account at any time.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

8. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and functional cookies for platform features. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: contact@communik.app
